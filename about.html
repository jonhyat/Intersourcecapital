OUR FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

How can Intersource Capital Limited help me get a funded trading account?

We partner with top prop firms like Funding Pips, Alpha Capital Group, FTMO, and 5%ers to help traders secure funded accounts. Our team provides trading mentorship, evaluation preparation, risk management strategies, and trade analysis to increase your chances of passing prop firm challenges.

What makes your trader recruitment and evaluation process unique?

Unlike other firms, we run an internal evaluation to assess traders before they attempt prop firm challenges. We offer personalized coaching, trading analytics, and strategy refinement to ensure traders have the best chance of securing funding and maintaining profitability.

Do you provide trading automation or AI-based trading solutions?

Yes! We develop custom trading bots, indicators, and algorithmic trading strategies to help traders optimize their performance. We also provide trading dashboards and analytics to track and improve trading results.

Can investors earn passive income with your fund management services?

Absolutely! We manage high-performance trading portfolios, allowing investors to earn profits through our profit-sharing investment programs. We apply strict risk management to ensure steady returns and capital growth.

What forex trading education and coaching do you offer?

We provide structured forex courses, one-on-one coaching, live trading sessions, and market breakdowns. Our education covers Smart Money Concepts (SMC), ICT strategies, liquidity concepts, and risk management to help traders master profitable trading techniques.
